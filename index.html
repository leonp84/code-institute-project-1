One Saturday to change your culinary world forever

Master the art and science of smoking perfect pork ribs

Understanding Low & Slow Cooking

Join us for a Saturday in Linz, where you'll learn the basics of tried-and-tested slow smoked baby back ribs. Why does smoking at low temperatures produce such perfection?

For Beginner and Pro

Whether an absolute newbie or a more experienced grill master, you’ll benefit from our unique approach to spicing, smoking and marinating ribs using everyday equipment.

Take Home Recipes

We’ll provide you with a variety of spice blend and marinade recipes to take home and try. Our recipes are old school classics and a few new out-of-the-box variants for adventurous eaters.

Let's have lunch!

After the class, students join the instructor for lunch at our outdoor location where our smoked meats and additional beverages will be provided. Lunch with an amazing view!
